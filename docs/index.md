# Keda Introduction

Welcome to the user documentation for **Keda**, your personal and family finance manager.

Keda helps you keep track of your expenses, share them with your household, and manage your budget effectively.

## Main Features

- **Consolidated Expenses**: View all your household expenses in one place.
- **Budget Feedback**: Visual indicators to know exactly how much you have left.
- **Household Sharing**: Manage finances together with your family or roommates.
- **Detailed Categories**: Organize spending with customizable categories.
