# Household Members

Keda is designed for families. You can share your financial data with other members of your home.

## Managing Members

Navigate to the **Members** tab to see who has access to the household data. 

![Members Screen](/assets/members.png)

## Roles and Permissions

All invited members have administrative privileges. They can view, add, edit, and delete expenses, as well as manage other household members.

## Inviting Members

In the **Members** tab, click **Invite Member** to generate an invitation link or send an invite via email.
