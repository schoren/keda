# Managing Expenses

Tracking your spending is the core of Keda.

## Adding an Expense

To add an expense, simply click on the category it belongs to on the dashboard, fill in the amount and note, and save.

## Expense List

On the dashboard, you can see the total spent per category. 

- **Filter**: Use the search bar for specific expenses.
- **Edit/Delete**: Long-press an expense in the detailed view to modify or remove it.
